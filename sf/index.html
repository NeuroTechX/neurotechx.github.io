About NeuroTechX

NeuroTechX brings hackers, enthusiasts and experts together to drive innovation and foster collaboration at local and international scales. Our movement’s core mission is to build a strong global neurotech community by providing key resources and learning opportunities, and being leaders in local and worldwide technological initiatives. We believe neurotechnology is key to better understanding and to improving who we are. Join us to take part in the conversation and help shape the future of neurotechnology with this bottom-up / DIY initiative ! For Neurotech Hackers, by Neurotech Hackers.

NeuroTechX has chapters in many cities around the world: NeuroTechMTL in Montréal, NeuroTechNYC in New York, NeuroTechParis in Paris, NeuroTechSF in San Francisco, NeuroTechTLV in Tel-Aviv, NeuroTechTO in Toronto, NeuroTechVA in Vancouver. Join us!
