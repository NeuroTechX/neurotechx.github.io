Content Creator & Curator (Remote)

NeuroTechX is looking for people who like to create and/or curate content about neurotechnology. The field is growing and lots of things are happening (e.g. science, opportunities, big news, ethics, industry, etc.) and we'd like to expose that information and engage discussions around it.

Type of content:

  • Tutorials (EDU)
    We are agregating and creating neurotech-related tutorials. (see EDU!)
  • Articles (Blog)
    We would like to have (semi)-regular posts on interesting & trending things in neurotechnology.
  • Presentations (Chapters)
    Nice (powerpoint) presentations on neurotechnology-related stuff for NTX Chapters to use.
  • Opportunities (Recruitment)
    We are 'mapping' the opportunities in the field (industry & academia) to facilitate recruitment.
  • Other
    We have many ideas we'd like to explore (MOOCs, Podcast, etc.)



*Please note this position is a volunteer (unpaid) position.


General Role & Responsibilities

  • Search Stuff
  • Write Stuff
  • Explain Stuff
  • Document & Organize Stuff

SKILLS & EXPERTISE

  • Good Writing Skills (English)
  • Good Communication Skills (e.g. ability to explain complex scientific concepts clearly and simply)
  • Ability to work with others (we're a community after all! :p)


*The expectations for NTX volunteer positions are ~5h / week.

About NeurotechX

NeuroTechX brings hackers, enthusiasts, researchers and experts together to drive innovation and foster collaboration at local and international scales. Our core mission is to build a strong global neurotech community by providing key resources, learning opportunities, and by being leaders in local and worldwide technological initiatives. We believe neurotechnology is key to better understanding and to improving who we are. Join us to take part in the conversation and help shape the future of neurotechnology!
