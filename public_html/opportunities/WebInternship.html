Web Dev - Paid Internship
(Part time - Montreal, Canada)

Under the Internship program for young people coordinated by Communautique



NeuroTechX is looking for 1 part time Web Developers (front-end) to work on an educational platform for neurotechnology. In an agile & motivated team, you will be working with people from Marketing, Operations, among others, to move the project forward and keep things on track. This role is for an ambitious someone with some good web skills to help NeuroTechX democratize and make education for neurotechnology accessible around the world.

You will work on a platform offering features like tutorials (e.g. here), projects (e.g. here), login system, user profiles, gamification system, etc.

This platform will become the 'go to' for everything related to neurotechnology, don't miss out on the opportunity to put such an achievement under your belt.


General Role & Responsibilities

  • Consult the team to define the needs
  • Develop the the UI/UX to make it as appealing & as intuitive as possible
  • Plan, design, write, modify, integrate and test
  • Write Code! Good Code. Scalable Code. Open Source Code.

Requirements & Expertise

  • Be aged 15-30 years
  • Have followed or follow preferable post-secondary courses in Information Technology
  • Be located in Montreal
  • Be eligible to work in Canada (canadian citizen or permanent residency)< /span>
  • Should not receive EI benefits while participating in training and not have already benefited from the program.
  • Front End Development experience
  • Understanding of current web platforms and languages

Conditions

  • Part-Time (~20h / sem)
  • Duration: 420 hours
  • Salary: 19,91$/h
  • Start date: As soon as possible.

If you are someone with experience in web development, as well as the desire to help our community grow, please send us your resume, a cover letter and a link to your profile or portfolio at yannick [at] neurotechx.com.

About NeurotechX

NeuroTechX brings hackers, enthusiasts, researchers and experts together to drive innovation and foster collaboration at local and international scales. Our core mission is to build a strong global neurotech community by providing key resources, learning opportunities, and by being leaders in local and worldwide technological initiatives. We believe neurotechnology is key to better understanding and to improving who we are. Join us to take part in the conversation and help shape the future of neurotechnology!
